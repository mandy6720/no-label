About

Mission Statement

No label mushrooms and microgreens arose from a desire to become directly involved with the tools and trades showing what we think to be the most promise in social, spiritual, and environmental health and happiness.

With a framework of love for gardening and cultivation, and a blessing of good friends and family, we've set out to follow in the footsteps of the worlds great mycological minds starting by bringing delicious, high quality gourmet mushrooms to the tables in the Leander/Austin TX area, and thereby creating an awareness for not only their taste and culinary uses, but limitless potential for greater health.

With this platform in place, we wish to expand our efforts into research in medicinal and environmental applications, as is being done by all the other inspiring figures in this movement.
